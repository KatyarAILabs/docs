Katyar provides production-grade tools to secure your AI agents with minimal changes to your existing code. Choose the right integration for your stack:

- [Python SDK](/integrations/python) – Full-featured, async-first SDK for most users  
- [TypeScript / JavaScript SDK](/integrations/typescript) – Modern Node.js / browser support  
- [Framework Wrappers](/integrations/frameworks) – LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen, and more  
- [CLI & Third-Party Tools](/integrations/cli-third-party) – Command-line management + Slack, webhooks, identity providers  

All SDKs connect via secure WebSocket to the Katyar gateway, enforce identity, policies, semantic firewall, and HITL automatically.
