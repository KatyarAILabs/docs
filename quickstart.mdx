Get your AI agent protected and visible in the Katyar dashboard in **under 10 minutes**.

Follow these steps exactly — no prior setup required.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [https://app.katyar.ai](https://app.katyar.ai)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Sign in with Google or GitHub
4. You'll land directly on your dashboard

## Step 2: Create a Workspace

Workspaces keep your agents, policies, and logs isolated (perfect for dev/staging/prod separation).

1. Click **Create Workspace**
2. Give it a name (e.g. `production`, `my-project`, `sandbox`)
3. Click **Create**

## Step 3: Get Your API Key

1. Go to **Settings → API Keys** (top-right menu)
2. Click **Create API Key**
3. Name it something memorable (e.g. `my-first-agent-key`)
4. Copy the key immediately — you won't see it again!
